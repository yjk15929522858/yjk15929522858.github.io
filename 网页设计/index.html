Captain America

Captain America is a veteran hero created in the era of World War II and one of the most famous anti fascist fighters. He was originally a thin young Steve Rogers. He underwent super soldier transformation surgery and became the only successful experiment. His physical quality reached the human limit. He has no real super power. Although he uses an indestructible special shield, his biggest weapon is courage, perseverance and patriotism. On the eve of the end of World War II, he fell asleep in the ice sea and was found by modern heroes decades later. The awakened Rogers continues to fight for justice, but the contemporary world is not as clear as it used to be. From time to time, he is regarded as a living fossil behind the times.
